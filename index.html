M.D.R.S.

In February 2022, I took part in a mission in the Mars Desert Research Station. It's a Mars Analog research facility in the Utah Desert. My 5 crewmates and I stayed 3 weeks in this facility without contact with the outside performing Extra Vehicular Activities and experiments for labs and companies.

During this mission, I was the crew's journalist. My misssion was to write reports of each day both in English and in French. This task was crucial because this was the only news our family had from us. I enjoyed so much writting those reports. You can find them all on the website of the mission.
