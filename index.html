Hi! My name is Pierre Fabre, I'm a French robotics control engineer working at Wandercraft on the walk of the first self-balancing personal exoskeleton. As mentionned before, my field is control theory. I'm a huge fan of the work of Brian Douglas the famous author of the Map of Control Theory. I like to solve problems whether it's solving boulder problems in a gym or outdoor in the Fontainebleau forest, solving chess puzzles on chess.com or even solving engineering problems for fun projects like the furuta pendulum we built with Armand du Parc.

In February 2022, I took part in a mission in the Mars Desert Research Station. It's a Mars Analog research facility in the Utah Desert. My 5 crewmates and I stayed 3 weeks in this facility without contact with the outside performing Extra Vehicular Activities and experiments for labs and companies. During this mission, I was the crew's journalist. My misssion was to write reports of each day both in English and in French. This task was crucial because this was the only news our family had from us. I enjoyed so much writting those reports. You can find them all on the website of the mission. We also have a YouTube channel you can check out!
